Finding A Group

Finding a group can be one of the hardest parts of commander. Finding good people to sit down and jam some commander with can be hard if you don't know where to look, but page will help you identify the many ways you can find people and places to play commander.


Local game stores, or LGS's are a great way to meet new people and jam some commander. There are many places you can find local game stores, such as within Utah, where LGS's are plentiful. Each store generally has set dates as to when tables are set aside to play commander. Here is a table of several LGS's in Utah, and their commander nights
